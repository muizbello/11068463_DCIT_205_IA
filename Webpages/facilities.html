Our Facilities

Explore Our state-of-the-art facilities.

Welcome to the Computer Science Department's Facilities Page! Here, we invite you to discover the technological wonders that form the backbone of our academic and research pursuits. Our commitment to providing an exceptional learning environment is reflected in our cutting-edge facilities, designed to empower students and researchers alike.

Our laboratories

The department leverages the computer capabilities of Balme library for students who
do not have personal computers. There are various sections of the library(Knowledge Commons, Korean Centre, Information Access Centre, Research Commons etc) that has computers with specs sufficient to handle most of the requirements of students

Collaboration Spaces

Engage in creative problem-solving and collaborative discussions in our designated collaboration spaces. Students have access to the research lab, hardware lab, and software lab which are well equipped with theses, dissertations and books on various technological concepts for their perusal.

Recommended Specifications

Although students have access to the computers at the libraries, it is recommended that each student have their own personal computers to ease their stay in the department. The recommended specifications include but are not limited to:
8/16 GB Ram
256 SSD/500 HDD
Good graphics capabilities.
