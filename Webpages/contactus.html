Where you can find us.

The department of Computer Science is situated opposite the Mathematics, Statistics, and Acturial Science departments building. The Math,Statistics, and Acturial science department is in turn to the left of the faculty of law. The department building is adjacent the Regional Institute of Population Studies(RIPS). The department's reception is on the right side of the ground floor of the building. All enquiries should first be made at the reception.
