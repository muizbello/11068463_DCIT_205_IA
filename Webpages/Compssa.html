Our Student Organisation.

Welcome to Our Community

The Computer Science Student Organization at University Of Ghana is a dynamic and inclusive community that brings together students passionate about technology, innovation, and collaboration. Our organization serves as a hub for networking, skill development, and fostering a sense of camaraderie among computer science enthusiasts.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to enhance the student experience within the Computer Science Department by:

  • Promoting Collaboration: Facilitating teamwork and collaboration among students through projects, hackathons, and coding challenges.
  • Professional Development: Providing opportunities for skill development, workshops, and guest lectures to prepare our members for success in the tech industry.
  • Community Engagement: Connecting with the wider community through outreach programs, technology-driven initiatives, and partnerships with local organizations.

What We Offer

Benefits of being a member of COMPSSA include:

  • Regular Meetings: Join us for our weekly or bi-weekly meetings where we discuss upcoming events, share industry insights, and provide a platform for networking.
  • Workshops and Tech Talks: Engage in hands-on workshops and attend tech talks by industry professionals to stay updated on the latest trends and technologies.
  • Hackathons and Coding Competitions: Participate in hackathons and coding competitions to apply your skills, solve real-world problems, and connect with like-minded peers.
  • Social Events: Enjoy social events, game nights, and outings that foster a sense of community and friendship among our members.

Get Involved

Whether you're a freshman exploring the world of computer science or a seasoned coder looking to expand your network, there's a place for you in our organization. Get involved by:

  1. Our Meetings: Check our schedule for upcoming meetings and join us to connect with fellow students.
  2. Participating in Events: Attend our workshops, hackathons, and social events to enrich your student experience.
  3. Contributing to Projects: Contribute your skills to ongoing projects and collaborate with peers on exciting initiatives.
  4. Leadership Opportunities: leadership roles within the organization to make a meaningful impact and develop your leadership skills.
