Why Choose Us?

At the Computer Science Department, we strive to foster a community of passionate individuals driven by a curiosity for technology and innovation. Our programs are designed to provide students with a solid foundation in computer science principles, hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies, and the skills needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving industry.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in I.T
  • MPhil. Computer Science
  • MsC. in Computer Science
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science

Admission Requirements

To join our vibrant community of learners, prospective students must meet the following general admission requirements:

  • Undergraduate Programs:
    • West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination(WASSCE), or
    • High School Diploma, or
    • Cambridge 'O' and 'A' levels.
  • Graduate Programs:
    • Bachelor's degree in a related field
